About Us


Who we are:

Our names are Daniel, Frank, Kimmy, and Sharon, and we are four UCI students who have formed friendships through long, stressful coding classes, extracurricular activities, and a mutual love for hotcakes and orange juice. We are a small, but quickly growing, start-up company that aims to cater to dietary needs of people living in urban and metropolitan city areas.

What we believe in:

Here at NOMz, we LOVE food. And when we say love. We mean LOVE. Therefore, we have a sincere belief that ingredients that food is prepared with should be grown and transported with quality, speed, and care.

Our mission:

We want you to be able to enjoy your food, so we aim to bring you top quality cuts and selections with speed and at an affordable price.
